About me

I'm currently a Project Assistant in the Control and Network Systems group at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, India. My research advisor is Dr. Pavankumar Tallapragada. I graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from R.V. College of Engineering, Bengaluru in 2018.

Research Interests

My research interests include coordination and motion planning for Autonomous Vehicles and robots, and human-robot interactions.
